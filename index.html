About the Artist

Hi, my name is Amber!

I am a tattoo artist who has been tattooing humans since Feb 2022. I work in Ballarat, VIC at a studio called Ballarat Tattoo Gallery.


I specialise in colour from cartoon characters to semi-realistic pieces or black and grey soft stipple. I pride myself in being able to excecute a range of styles. I particularly enjoy whimsical and old/vintage art designs!


To get in contact with me, fill out the form below with your ideas and I will be in touch via email! Or if instagram/facebook is easier for you, just search Ambz Tattz and you will find me!
